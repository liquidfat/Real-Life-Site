Diary Of MJ

It has been 254,681 years since I arrived here on Earth. My life so far has been a blessing, but I have also been living a lie. It seems that my most recent disguise, that of a pop superstar, could not have been more convincing. For years now I have been living it up, riding merry go rounds, buying primates as pets and singing for the amusement of billions but I fear I can do it no more.

These past decades my power has been diminishing greatly and I can feel my mortal coil leaving this body. My search for a protege and a true succesor has been fruitless so far, but I know I am near. Through the creation of Real Life tm I have been able to build a community of followers who want no more than to be as I am, all-powerful and all-knowing.

I can only hope that by the time I no longer exist, someone has managed to find this page. Using the holy texts of HTML and CSS I have hidden the key to my Godhood here. Whoever finds it is no doubt worthy of the power that brings. As long as they can contain it, they can protect Earth as I did for so many years..
